About the IMS

A shared system, built in the open.

The Local Gov IMS gives councils a flexible, modern way to manage income — payments, reconciliation, and accounting across departments — without the cost and complexity of proprietary systems.

What it does
  • Manages payments across all council departments
  • Handles bank reconciliation and suspense automatically
  • Supports card, cheque, and online payment channels
  • Full audit trail and reporting
  • Configurable to your council's fund codes and workflows
How it's built
  • Open source — GNU AGPLv3 licence
  • Built and maintained by partner councils
  • WCAG 2.1 AA accessible as standard
  • Developed in the open, in collaboration with MHCLG
  • Managed service available via Tailwind Digital

Features

What the IMS includes.

Designed as a minimum viable system, meeting the priority needs of councils. View all features →

Dashboard

Real-time overview of the system — import processing status and payment summaries at a glance.

Transaction search

All payments in real-time with powerful filtering, including narrative search for sort codes and account numbers.

Payments

Card and cheque recording for call centre and post room. Integrates with call management software.

Imports & suspense

Automatic bank file allocation. Unallocated items go to suspense with configurable processing rules to reduce them.

Audit trail

Full payment history, receipts by email or print, refunds, journals, and fund reallocation.

Configurable

Fund codes, VAT codes, payment methods — all configurable to your council's requirements.
