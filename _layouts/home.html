About Local Gov IMS

A shared system, built in the open.

The Local Gov Income Management System gives councils a flexible, modern way to recieve payments and manage income across departments, without the cost and complexity of proprietary systems.

What it does
  • Manages payments across all council departments
  • Handles back office income management functions with ease
  • Supports card, cheque, telephone, and online payments
  • Full audit trail and flexible reporting dashboard
  • Configurable to your council's fund codes and workflows
How it's built
  • Open source — GNU AGPLv3 licence
  • Originally developed by Barnsley using .NET framework 4.8.1 now upgraded to .NET core 10
  • WCAG 2.1 AA accessible as standard
  • Developed in the open, in collaboration with MHCLG
  • Managed service available via Tailwind Digital

Features

What Local Gov IMS includes.

Payments and income management designed by councils, for councils, with flexible modern tools. View all features →

Dashboard

Real-time overview of the system with import processing status and payment summaries at a glance.

Transaction search

All payments in real-time with powerful filtering, including narrative search for sort codes and account numbers.

Payments

Integrated payments across all methods including online, MOTO, cheque, in person via cash, card or cheque.

Imports & exports

Automatic file distributions, with configurable processing rules to process transactions automatically.

Audit trail

Full payment history, receipts by email or print, refunds, journals, and fund reallocation.

Configurable

Fund codes, VAT codes, payment methods — all configurable to your council's requirements.
